1. Contare quanti iscritti ci sono stati ogni anno

SOLUZIONE
SELECT COUNT(`id`) AS 'number_of_enrolments',YEAR(`enrolment_date`) 'year'
FROM `students`
GROUP BY YEAR(`enrolment_date`)  
ORDER BY `year`  ASC
